Constructs a neuron with a specified number of outputs.

Neuron constructor that initializes output weights with random values.

Parameters
numOutputsThe number of outputs for the neuron.
myIdxThe index of the neuron in its layer.

Each neuron is connected to neurons in the next layer, and these connections are initialized with random weights in the range [0, 1). Random initialization is essential for neural networks to avoid symmetry during training.
